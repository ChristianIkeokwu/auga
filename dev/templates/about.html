About auga

As members of Oberlin Investment Club, it is normal to see people coming to the club with ethusiasm, while leaving with dissapointment simply because coding skills are required to deal with data analyses.

In order to make investment a more accessible thing for everyone, we decide to build Auga, in order to benefit more people who are interested. Auga is a tool to visualize stock changes, and analyze basic data of different stocks, to help people to make the best decisions when they decide to make an investment.

Make investment an easy decision to make

Stocks are hard to predict, even with the tools created based on statistic models. Auga is created to make the make investment more accessible to people, and help people make better choices.
